About

InfoPost is dedicated to solving the modern day problems of fake news, headline misinformation, and low reading times plaguing the media today. We tackle these challenges by adopting cutting-edge AI for Natural Language Understanding, graphics generation, and human-centered design.

A San Diego, California based startup, InfoPost changes the way people experience the news by giving credibility scores and AI generated infographics which increases the clarity and transparency of the news.

Finally, InfoPost also provides corporate solutions by providing reliable credibility checks using Advanced AI and on-demand infographics for any purposes. It has applications for education, social media, politics, government and more.
