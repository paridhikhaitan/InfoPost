About InfoPost

A startup dedicated to solving the modern day problems of fake news, headline misinformation, and low reading times plaguing the media today. InfoPost tackles these challenges by adopting cutting-edge AI for Natural Language Understanding, art generation, and human-centered design.
A San Diego, California based startup. This company is trying to redefine the way people experience their news in our modern world by providing news in beautiful AI generated infographics, which make it easier to read, understand and digest.
Finally, InfoPost is dedicated currently for financial news, to simplify the jargon in the media world and make it digestible for the common man.
